Fontaine

The Nation of Hydro

Welcome for Fontaine. The nation of Hydro is now accessible for all to explore. Dive in and experience its rich culture as well as the story from the Archon Quest released from version 4.0 and beyond. Take on all new types of enemies. Explore the region and uncover its secrets. Take your adventure both above and underwater and experience the breathtaking sceneries of Fontaine. And of course, don't forget to befriend the Melousines, the native creatures of Fontaine.
